CARI-CREATURES

Event Rules:

  • Participant has to create a caricature of a famous personality on the condition that it should not depict that person’s real profession. It should, in some way depict him/her in some other profession.
  • The contest begins on August 1st week and the closing date for receipt of entries is extended to October 2nd week All dates are subject to change.
  • To enter the contest, send the entries to the email id announced through our social media platforms.
  • Participants can send multiple entries, however, only 1 will be entertained.
  • The participant should mention their name and college name in the mail.

Judging Criteria:

  • Drawing skills
  • Creativity
  • the transformation that has been brought to the person’s
  • personality/profession.
