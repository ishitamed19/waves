LET'S NOT GET WASTED

Event Rules:

  • Theme: Open
  • Various waste items will be auctioned under different categories viz., General, Mystery, etc.
  • Teams must bid for the items in ’minutes’. The auctioned item will be awarded to the highest bidder. The number of minutes bid by the highest bidder will be deducted from the team’s total of 90 minutes.
  • The Mystery item should be the centre of attraction of the model.
  • Further details to be announced on-spot.
  • Stationery to be brought by the participants.

Judging criteria:

  • Creativity.
  • Usage of the mystery item.
  • Other parameters and weightage to be decided by the judges.
