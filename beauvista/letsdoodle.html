LET'S DOODLE

Event Rules:

  • The Contest begins on October 1, 2018 and the closing date for receipt of entries is October 26, 2018 (“Contest Period”).
  • The contestant is required to use the Waves official logo in their doodle art (logo of any year would do).
  • To enter the Contest, send the entries by scanning them and posting them to the email id announced through our social media platforms.
  • Only one entry per participant will be entertained.
  • The participant should mention their name and college name in the mail.

Judging Criteria:

  • Drawing skills
  • Creativity
  • Originality - considering use of the Waves 2018 theme, as well as
  • the unique and novel approach to the Doodle.
