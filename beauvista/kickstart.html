KICK ST*ART*

Event Rules:

  • Theme to be announced on-spot.
  • In prelims, the teams have to paint the same design as that of the main event on an A-3 sheet using poster colours/water colours and markers (basically give the concept or the design).
  • A number of teams will be selected for Round 2 wherein they design the sneaker.
  • A pair of white sneaker will be provided to each of the teams.
  • Colours will be provided however teams can bring their own paints.
  • Basic stationary to be brought by the teams.
  • Teams have to model the sneaker designed in Round 2 on one of their team members and present it to the judges.

Judging criteria for Round 1:

  • Aesthetic Appeal
  • Creativity.
  • Other parameters and weightage to be decided by the judges

Judging Criteria for Round 2:

  • Sneaker presentation.
  • Incorporation of the theme.
  • Other parameters and weightage to be decided by the judges.
